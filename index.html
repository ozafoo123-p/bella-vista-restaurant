Bella Vista Restaurant — Business Website (Portfolio Project) Bella Vista is a fully responsive, single-page restaurant website built as a portfolio demo showcasing an elegant, upscale dining brand presence. It uses only HTML, CSS, and vanilla JavaScript, with no frameworks or backend, keeping it lightweight and fast. Key Sections: Hero Section — Full-screen intro with background image, tagline, and call-to-action button linking to reservations. Our Story — Short brand narrative explaining the restaurant's philosophy and ingredients sourcing. Signature Dishes — Card-based menu highlighting three standout dishes with pricing. Gallery — Visual showcase of the restaurant's interior and plated dishes. Reservation Form — Functional booking form with name, contact info, date, and party size (front-end only, doesn't send real data). Design Approach: An elegant, dark and gold color palette with serif typography reflects a fine-dining aesthetic. Built with Flexbox and CSS Grid for full responsiveness across devices — no external frameworks used. Note: This is a demo/portfolio project to demonstrate front-end design and deployment skills. Bella Vista is not a real business, and the reservation form does not send or store data.
